How does this work?

Choose An Image

Take a picture of your unwanted electronic and upload it. Make sure that the picture is clear and the product can be seen.

Add A Description

Based on what your electronic product is, add a description of what it is. Add details like what is the product , it's condition and any other relevant information.

Post It!

After choosing your picture and adding a description, simply post it! You can view yours and other people's listings under "Marketplace".





Frequently Asked Questions

Do we have to meet up with someone to donate?

No you do not. It is similar to Carousel, however, all you need to do is drop your electronic into the box at the designated location.

Where can we donate our electronic products?

The box for donation will be placed inside Capstone Project room at Blk 31 Level 5.

What do we get for donating?

There will be a QR code for you to scan to attain CCA points for participation. Donors will be automatically entered into a giveaway where you stand a chance to win a gift card of your choice at the end of the semester.

Can we just take any item inside the box?

Yes you can, however it incurs a small token fee of $3-$8; prices vary depending on the type of product being taken.
